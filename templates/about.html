About Us

Welcome to our student marketplace! This platform was created to help university students save money, exchange study materials, and find what they need easily.

Our mission is to make it simple and fast for students to sell, donate, and share used books, laptops, calculators, notebooks, and all study-related items.

Why We Created This Website?

  • Students struggle with high prices of new equipment.
  • Facebook / WhatsApp groups are not organized.
  • We want a clean, safe place for student-to-student exchange.
  • To support students with communication through a built-in chat.

We hope this platform will serve all students and make academic life easier.
